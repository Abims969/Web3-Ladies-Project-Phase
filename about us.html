OUR MISSION Quality Standard of Life For Orphans
The mission of Succor Homes is to provide a quality standard of living for orphans. We strive to provide a safe and loving environment for all children in our care. We will work tirelessly to ensure that every child has the opportunity to reach their full potential.
OUR VISION To Help Over 100,000 Orphans
To empower every orphan through skill acquisition so they can lead successful and fulfilling lives.
